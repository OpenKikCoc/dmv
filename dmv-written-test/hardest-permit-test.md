# DMV Practice Notes (Formatted)

## [2025 California Hardest Permit Test](https://www.dmv-written-test.com/california/practice-test-26.html)

> [!NOTE]
>


**17. You must file a report of a traffic accident occurring in California when:**
- Your vehicle fails a smog test.
- <span style="color:#16a34a">✅ You are involved in a collision and there is an injury.</span>
- You change your insurance company.

> When you have a collision, report it to DMV within 10 days if anyone was injured (no matter how slightly) or killed. You or your representative must make this report whether or not you caused the collision, even if the collision occurred on private property. 发生交通事故后，如有任何人受伤（无论轻重）或死亡，您或您的代表必须在10天内向车辆管理部门（DMV）报告。无论您是否是事故责任方，即使事故发生在私人土地上，您或您的代表也必须提交此报告。
